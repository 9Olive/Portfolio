Who am I?

I am a currently a Masters student at North Carolina State University studying Statistics with a focus on data science and machine learning applications. Prior to NCSU I studied at Barton College, and graduated in 2014 with a Bachelor's of Science in Chemistry and Mathematics. For the past three years I explored the chemistry side of my degree as an Analytical Chemist. With my lab coat dawned, I had formal training in a cGMP environment to execute and develop test methods for research compounds, clinical phase trials, and commercial drug products. Due to the pace and volume at which data moves, I felt compelled to incorporate my mathematical training as well. I have since taken a keen interest in the challenges and techniques used to manipulate big data to extract meaningful conclusions.
